NgForm is a directive in Angular that automatically creates and manages a form when we use a <form> tag in Template-Driven Forms.

It provides:

  • Tracks form state (valid, invalid, touched, dirty)
  • Holds all form controls and their values
  • Helps in form submission handling

Example:

<form #myForm="ngForm">
  <input name="username" ngModel>
</form>

In short:

NgForm represents the entire form and manages its state in template-driven forms.
