Validators.min() and Validators.max()

Validators.min() and Validators.max() are used for numeric range validation.

🔹 TypeScript 

age: new FormControl('', [
  Validators.min(18),
  Validators.max(60)
])

🔹 Validation Results

Input Result
15 ❌ Invalid
25 ✅ Valid
70 ❌ Invalid

Here allowed age range is 18 to 60.
