Validators.email() vs email HTML Attribute

Validators.email is Angular validation for email format.

🔹 Reactive Form 

email: new FormControl('', [
  Validators.email
])

🔹 Template-Driven Form 

<input
  type="email"
  name="email"
  ngModel
>

🔹 Difference

Validators.email type="email"
Angular validation Browser HTML validation
Works in reactive forms Works in HTML forms
Integrated with Angular errors Native browser validation
