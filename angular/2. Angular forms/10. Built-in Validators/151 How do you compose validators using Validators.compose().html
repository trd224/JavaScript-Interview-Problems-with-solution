Validators.compose() in Angular

Validators.compose() is used to combine multiple validators into one validator function.

🔹 TypeScript

username: new FormControl(
  '',
  Validators.compose([
    Validators.required,
    Validators.minLength(3),
    Validators.maxLength(10)
  ])
)

Here username is required, minimum 3 characters, and maximum 10 characters.
